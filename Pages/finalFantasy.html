Final Fantasy Crystal Chronicles

Players take on the role of adventurers who travel in a caravan gathering mystical fuel for crystals which protect the world's settlements from the destructive Miasma. The single-player campaign has the player escort the vessel carrying the crystal's energy, defending it from enemies and solving puzzles to progress.

Final Fantasy Tactics

Final Fantasy Tactics is a tactical role-playing game (RPG) set in the fictional medieval world of Ivalice during a time of war. Players follow the story of Ramza Beoulve, a noble trying to stop an upcoming war between warring families and engage in battles on isometric, three-dimensional fields. The game features two modes of play: battles and the world map.
